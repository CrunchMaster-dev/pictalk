Parent settings

Mode

Choose how this device talks. Picture board for a child who taps pictures; Keyboard for someone who can read and type.

Switch scanning

For someone who can't touch the screen accurately. The device highlights items one at a time; press a switch — the spacebar, Enter, or a tap anywhere — to select. Works with plug-in assistive switches too.

Quick phrases

One-tap sentences for the keyboard. Tapping one speaks it instantly — great for urgent or common things.
